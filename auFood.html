Tasty and Yummy Food

in Assumption University

The reviews of interesting and amazing food that can be found around Assumption University Suvarnabhumi Campus

Restaurant Reviews

Jippy House

Location

Assumption University Suvarnabhumi Campus, AU Mall 1st floor

Restaurant Rating

Service: 4.5/5

Food: 5/5

Cleanliness: 4/5

Price: 3.5/5

Overall Rating: 4.5/5

Jippy House is one most popular restaurant in Assumption University. It might be the restaurant that lasts the longest AU Mall. The dishes in this restaurant are very pleasing, the restaurant is well-organized, good service-minded staff, and short waiting time. However, the prices in this restaurant are quite too high for me, but it is understandable as dishes are quite big, which is not suitable for a non-eater like me. Anyway, overall is quite good.

Recommended Dishes

Grilled Pork Neck

This is my favourite menu in this restaurant. It is not spicy and the sauce is pretty good.

Price: 79 Baht

Green Curry with Chicken

The taste of this menu is pretty strong, it's a bit too spicy for me and the sweetness is perfect.

Price: 69 Baht

Chicken with Scrambled Eggs

This is the best menu in the restaurant. The chickens and scrambled eggs are very perfect.

Price: 69 Baht


IM FiN FiN

Location

Assumption University Suvarnabhumi Campus, AU Mall 1st floor

Restaurant Rating

Service: 3/5

Food: 4.5/5

Cleanliness: 4.5/5

Price: 4/5

Overall Rating: 4/5

The overall of this IM FiN FiN restaurant is good, the prices are reasonable, but the waiting time is quite a bit too long. Sometimes if there are many people, it could take up to 40 minutes to serve. The tastes of dishes are pretty decent, most of the menus are okay. What I dislike is that sometimes there are arguements between staff and owners in the restaurant, but when it comes to servicing, staffs are very polite. Prices are also reasonable.

Recommended Dishes

Roasted Chicken with Hot Sauce

This is my favourite menu of this restaurant, the sacue is not too spicy and chicken is well-roasted.

Price: 69 Baht

Stir-fried Noodles with Chicken

This menu is pretty decent, stir-fried noodles is well done. Not spicy and not bland.

Price: 59 Baht

Suki Yaki Soup

This menu is a bit too spicy, but I love that they put eggs in there. Good menu to order.

Price: 69 Baht


AU Cafeteria

Location

Assumption University Suvarnabhumi Campus, AU Mall 1st and 2nd floor

Restaurant Rating

Service: 4/5

Food: 4/5

Cleanliness: 3/5

Price: 5/5

Overall Rating: 4/5

AU Cafetria is where I eat the most. Even though it is not very clean here, but there are variety of foods you can choose and most importantly, they are much cheaper than the other restaurants, you can keep your budget for any other things else. AU Cafeteria is located in both 1st floor and 2nd floor of AU Mall, which there are different kinds of restaurants available.Before going there, you need to exchange your money to money tickets to buy dishes in the cafeteria at the front desk.

Recommended Dishes

Chicken Biryani

My favourite one! A bit spicy which is perfect and the chicken is crispy.

Price: 50 Baht

Rice with Egg, Hotdog, and Tofu

The hotdog is pretty tasty, other things are normal. But I enjoy it with this price!

Price: 45 Baht

Fried Chicken Rice

The chickens are very very crispy! Recommended for fried chicken lovers with a very cheap price

Price: 40 Baht

Fried Zucchini and Sausage

This menu is recommeneded for sausage lovers. Zucchini makes the dish not too dried.

Price: 45 Baht

Rice with Omelet

The basic menu. If you don't know what to eat, pick this. It can fulfill your lunch for sure.

Price: 50 Baht

Fried Egg with Tofu Carrot

The fried egg is half done which is great with the tofu carrot puts more moist to the dish.

Price: 45 Baht
